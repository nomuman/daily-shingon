# Privacy Policy (Daily Shingon)

Last updated: 2025-12-29

## 1. Introduction

Daily Shingon (the “App”) is a daily practice companion inspired by Shingon Buddhism.
This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

At this time, the App does not collect or transmit personal information or usage data to external servers.

## 3. On-Device Storage

Practice logs, settings, and related data are stored locally on your device.
This data is not synced to any external server.

## 4. Notifications

The App uses notification permission to provide morning/night reminders.
You can turn notifications on/off and adjust times in the App and/or your device settings.

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure

The App does not share your data with third parties.

## 6. Third-Party Services

At this time, the App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
If we add any third-party service in the future, we will clearly disclose:

- the service name,
- what data may be processed,
- the purpose,
  and we will update this policy.

## 7. External Links

The App may contain external links (e.g., references and sources).
Information handling on those sites is governed by their own policies.

## 8. Children

The App is not designed specifically for children.
Guardians may use device-level controls as needed.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed.
If changes are significant, we will provide notice in the App and/or on the public policy page.
