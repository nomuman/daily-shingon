## Full Description (Long)

Daily Shingon is a short daily practice companion inspired by Shingon Buddhism.
It supports everyday learning and practice in simple steps—without replacing religion or formal training.

Core features

- Morning (3 min): a brief check-in to align body, speech, and mind
- Midday (30 sec): one learning card to turn insight into one action today
- Night (45 sec): reflect and close the day with a gentle routine
- Supportive guidance to return after breaks (no streak pressure)
- Reminders for morning/night rhythm
- Learning cards + glossary to deepen understanding
- Logs are stored locally on your device (no sync)

Principles

- No guarantees or “results” claims
- No criticism of other traditions or people
- No instructions for specialized/advanced ritual procedures

Privacy

- Personal logs are stored on your device and are not synced to external servers
- No analytics or ad SDKs are used at this time (we will update this page if added)
