About

Daily Shingon is a gentle entry point for daily practice. It is not a substitute for religion, but a small, clear routine you can return to.

Daily rhythm

3 minutes in the morning, 30 seconds at midday, 45 seconds at night.

Morning | Align

~3 minutes

Settle body, speech, and mind, then pick one simple act for today.

Midday | Learn

~30 seconds

A short learning card turns ideas into a concrete action.

Night | Close

~45 seconds

Confession → vow → dedication to close the day quietly.

Three axes: body, speech, mind

These three axes stay visible throughout the experience.

Body | Posture & action
Speech | Mantra & words
Mind | Contemplation
