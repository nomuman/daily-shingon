About

Daily Shingon is a small, steady companion that helps bring learning into everyday practice. It aims to be quiet, clear, and easy to return to.

Daily rhythm

Morning | Align
~3 minutes. Settle body, speech, and mind.
Midday | Learn
~30 seconds. Turn a short idea into one act.
Night | Close
~45 seconds. Confession → vow → dedication.

Three axes

Body, speech, and mind remain visible throughout.

What we value

  • Short, clear guidance
  • A return-friendly flow
  • Respectful, non-absolute language
  • Logs remain on your device
